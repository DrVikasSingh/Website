Dr. Vikas Singh

Economist, Entrepreneur, Financial Analyst

About



Dr. Vikas Singh is a social entrepreneur and founder of Crux Management.

Dr. Singh consults for large businesses and governments on governance and strategy. He reaches many more leaders through his speaking engagements across the world.

The Indian School of Business, Institute of Public Enterprise, UNICEF, ILO, GIZ, UNDP, and MCRHRD are part of Dr. Singh’s associations across the globe. Highly sought by both the policy makers and the political leaders, his engagements are focused on helping leaders in the industry and government face the challenges of growth, governance, and leadership.

Known for his advocacy of inclusive growth and development, Dr. Singh’s work in the area of financial inclusion and sustainable development has created sustainable livelihood opportunities and impacted over millions of Indians. He continues supporting the inclusive ecosystem and working for the less privileged.

Dr. Singh is a widely read columnist and writes for Business World, Dainik Jagran, Eenadu, and other publications. He is widely translated in other languages and is frequently seen on the electronic media presenting his views on the economy and policy.

Dr. Singh’s TED talk ‘Happiness is Both Social and Personal; in India it is Mostly Economic’ addresses the issue and challenges of inequality. He is largely credited with taking the idea of Universal basic Income narrative in the public domain.
