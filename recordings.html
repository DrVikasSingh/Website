Universal Basic Income @ TEDxHyderabad


Inclusion cannot be achieved by unidirectional thinking. Inclusion makes for a stronger economy. It is a multidimensional topic and there are so many different way to achieve inclusion. One highly debated topic is that of Universal Basic Income (UBI). Dr. Vikas Singh addresses the issue of inclusion and the impact of UBI. He talks about the pros and cons of implementing UBI in a hugely diverse country like India. He cites examples from countries implementing UBI, talking about how these policies are needed to stress on a growth that is inclusive. He debates the challenges thrown by this concept, yet appeals to policy makers to look at implementing UBI, as he says there are more advantages than disadvantages.
